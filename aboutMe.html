"I have no special talents. I am only passionately curious." - Albert Einstein

I've been a teacher. I've been a student. I've been a barista. I've been a traveler. I've been a technician. Now, I am all of these and I keep adding to my personal profile. I consider myself first and foremost a traveler though. I love going to new places and experiencing new things for life is not meant to be lived in one spot. Perhaps, it's because I grew up in a small town in Ebensburg PA, my love for traveling is insatiable. Every time I turn static, I constantly devise ways to hit the road again. Peru is my next destination. I love hiking and being on the mountains. So my life doesn't feel complete until I've gone to Machu Picchu.
